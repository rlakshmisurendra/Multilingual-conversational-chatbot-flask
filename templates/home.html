Multilingual Conversational Chatbot

Smart AI system that supports many Indian & global languages

UniLingo is an advanced multilingual conversational AI chatbot powered by Google's Gemini AI. It automatically detects your input language and responds in the same language, providing clear, concise, and contextually relevant answers. Whether you're asking questions in English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, or any other supported language, UniLingo adapts seamlessly to deliver an intelligent conversational experience.
